Last updated: 2026-04-13

1. Who we are

Asset Tracker is a tool for recording and viewing personal investment assets, portfolio snapshots, retirement planning, and related calculations.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: mike.zhang3679@gmail.com

2. Scope of this policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Asset Tracker application and related subscription, restore purchase, and support flows.

3. Information we collect

You may choose to enter information such as:

  • Asset names, asset types, quantities, prices, currencies, and notes
  • Manual exchange rates
  • Historical snapshots
  • Retirement plan and balance settings
  • Investment financing records
  • Backup files that you export or import

For subscriptions, Apple and our subscription provider may return:

  • Subscription product identifiers
  • Whether the subscription is active
  • Expiration dates
  • Restore purchase results

4. How we use information

  • To display and manage your asset data on your device
  • To calculate totals, trends, beta, retirement projections, and balance suggestions
  • To support subscriptions, purchases, and restore purchases
  • To improve app stability and meet platform compliance requirements

5. Data storage

The current version of Asset Tracker is primarily local-first. Core business data is generally stored on your device, and we do not proactively upload your asset records to our own servers in the current version.

If you use backup export, the exported files may contain sensitive financial information. You are responsible for storing those files safely.

6. Third-party services

We may rely on third-party services such as:

  • Apple App Store / In-App Purchase
  • RevenueCat for subscription status handling
  • Public market data or exchange-rate providers

7. We do not sell your personal information

We do not sell your personal information.

8. Data retention and deletion

Because the current version stores most business data locally, you can manage or remove most data by deleting local records, uninstalling the app, or deleting exported backup files.

9. Children’s privacy

Asset Tracker is not designed for children and is not intended to collect children’s personal information.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes may be reflected in app updates, release notes, or this page.
